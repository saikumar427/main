Follow these steps to add Merchant ID, Publick Key and Private Key in Payment Settings section of Eventbee.

Step 1:
In your Braintree admin console, visit Account -> Click On My user in the drop down list


Step 2:
Scroll down the page to Authorization section at the bottom of page. Click On API Keys of Authorization section.


Step 3:
Click on View under Private Key section


Step 4:
Enter the corresponding rounded values i.e;Merchant ID, Public Key and Private Key in text boxes of Payment Settings page in eventbee.
